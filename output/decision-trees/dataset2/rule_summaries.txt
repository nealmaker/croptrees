## BM
**Base rule (2% discount rate):**
- With crown ratio >= 30%: DBH <= 17"
- With crown ratio < 30%: DBH <= 15"
- **Veneer bonus:** Veneer-quality trees can be +2" larger

**Discount rate adjustment:** Max DBH decreases by ~1" for each 1% increase in discount rate

**Site adjustments:**
- granitictill: -0.8" to max DBH

## YB
**Base rule (2% discount rate):** Crop tree if DBH <= 26" when crown ratio >= 40%, or DBH <= 17" when crown ratio < 40%
*Note: Trees with better crowns (higher CR) can be retained at larger sizes.*

**Discount rate adjustment:** Max DBH decreases by ~2.3" for each 1% increase in discount rate

**Site adjustments:**
- richnhw: +2.5" to max DBH
- clayplain: +1.5" to max DBH
- mountaintill: -1.5" to max DBH
- granitictill: -2.5" to max DBH
